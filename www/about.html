About Me

I was born and raised in South Jersey; about 15 minutes from Philadelphia. I grew up drawing and designing anything I could think of, I guess you could say my childhood talent was drawing. As I grew, so did my artistic talent and love for being creative. Throughout all my years in school, I was heavily involved in my art classes and was learning new techniques and building my talent.

In my early High School years, my creative side took a turn towards music. I started to play drums, guitar and writing songs. Before you knew it, I was in a band and we were part of the South Jersey punk scene. After over 10 years of playing music and in and out of bands, I decided to take a break from the scene as everyone, including myself, was "growing up" and had jobs or other priorities to attend to.

I still love to play and write music in my free time, but then again, my creative side took another turn to writing. I recently wrote a 130 page screenplay. I still work on it to get it just right, as I'm a perfectionist, while I try to get it the exposure I feel it deserves.

In 2010 I decided to go back to school to fulfill a passion I had with technology and computers. And one field that always snagged my attention was Web and Graphic Design. So come June, I was going to Full Sail University for Web Design & Development. And I walked away with a broad knowledge of the field. I was taught design tools such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Flash and Premier. Then Client-Side languages such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and jQuery. I also learned Server-Side Programming using MAMP that includes PHP and MySQL. While I was learning these useful tools, I was taught the ins and outs of the production phases, such as conducting client surveys and building wireframes, site maps and design comps, all the way to the launch phase.

.....I'm just ready to be pushed to no limits.
